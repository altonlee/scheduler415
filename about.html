Final Product

A scheduler app for your scheduling needs.
Uses JQuery for the fancy stuff, Semantic UI for styling, and DayPilot for the calendar.
Since I used their Lite version, a lot of capabilities were locked, and I had to make a LOT of modifications and backward programming methods.
Overall, I hope you enjoy this app.

Presentations

The presentation should be professional - imagine presenting it at a large gathering of other professionals, e.g. at a company-wide or academic conference - and should therefore have the following format:

  • A title slide with the title of the presentation, the names of (all) project member(s) and their affiliation.
  • An introductory slide with brief description of the project (what you will tell us, typically an intro to background and goal).
  • The body (that what you want to tell us in detail)
  • A conclusion slide (what you told us, typically summary of most important points)
These concepts should be embedded in a PowerPoint (or PowerPoint-like) presentation and published within the your folder on Google Drive as 'Slides'.
